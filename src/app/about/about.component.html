Pacific Shores Shuttle


I'm Frances and I built this app so that if you work at Pacific Shores like I do, you can finally figure out when the next shuttle is without pondering over a table.


Relatable

I work at Pacific Shores myself and take public transporation from time to time.

Reliable

All info was taken directly from the Caltrain website so you know it's on point.

Responsive

It was designed to be mobile friendly, easy on the eyes, and simple to use.
